By Beverly Clark on July 11, 2015
Format: Kindle Edition Verified Purchase
I purchased this book as a read aloud bedtime story for my two grandchildren. While my granddaughter, age 7, is a good listener, my grandson, age 2, is a bit more rambunctious. He was absolutely glued to this story from start to finish and we will be re-reading it again this evening. My granddaughter also really enjoyed the story.

Snowpo was of course, absolutely delightful and we loved his antics.
I'd like to see more books by this author.

Delightful and unique children's book
By TFLReader TOP 500 REVIEWER on July 17, 2015
Format: Kindle Edition Verified Purchase
I read a lot of books with my young niece and she is pretty much a fan of anything with pictures and rhyming. We just finished reading "Snowpo" and we both simply loved this delightful tale. The story of a little polar bear, Popo, who goes to live in London with his family and his brother the seal. The two have some fun adventures but are also pursued by a kilt-wearing porcupine ranger. This was a delightful read for both of us that had wonderful rhyming and some of the best illustrations in a children's book that I have seen in a long time. Definitely recommend.
