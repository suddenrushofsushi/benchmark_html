I'd love to have an option to offer a spot for a user to write something about me on my profile, to help find a good group when on the lfg forums.  I think it would have to be an opt-in thing for users, and perhaps comments must be approved by the user before going on their page.  Alternatively, a standard of tags that people can apply to a user, Silly, Tactical, Dialogue Heavy, Descriptive, Voice Acting, Serious, Easygoing, Battle Oriented, Punctual, things like that. After 'x' people tag you with a trait, it goes on your profile.  People want very different things from their role playing experiences, and I'd love a way to find like-minded folks to invite to my groups.
