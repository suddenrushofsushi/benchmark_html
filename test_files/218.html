The Four Foundations of Mindfulness: Meditation Techniques for Joyful Living

Taught by Andy Quinn

What to expect: Mindfulness meditation techniques that improve mood difficulties such as anger, anxiety and depression, lower distractibility and impulsivity, and relieve stress. Techniques are extremely useful in relationships. Sessions will be taught in a safe atmosphere. Time will be devoted to instruction, practice and question and answer.

Who: Beginners and experienced people are welcome.

When: Mondays, August 18 & 25, September 8 & 15, 2014, 7 – 9 PM

Where: The office of Andy Quinn, 215 E.Bay St., Suite 5, Lakeland FL 33801

Fee: The teacher takes no fee. Students are free to make a contribution to the teacher out of gratitude for the teaching.

Please register in advance. Email Andy Quinn or  call him at 863-683-9600.

Andy has been practicing Vipassana Meditation for twenty-one years. He has studied with Peter Carlson, and has attended and served numerous long Vipassana courses taught in the style of S.N Goenka. He has also practiced at Insight Meditation Society in Barre, Massachusetts. He is the founder of the Lakeland Sangha in Polk County Florida and has hosted numerous one-day courses taught by Peter in Polk County. Andy is currently receiving Teacher Mentoring Training with Peter. Andy is a private practice therapist in Lakeland specializing in addictions, trauma and marriage.
