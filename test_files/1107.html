Event Details

LOB's Brooklyn 'X' Factor is a Live Music Show with Audience Participation. With the tagline, "Sing, Sang, Sung", some of the best area singers will get the opportunity to showcase their best vocal performance to our celebrity music panel and a live crowd who will get to Vote for their Favorite singer of the night. 

With the hope of gaining exposure and the attention of our guest music executives, media personalities, sponsors or taking home over $10,000 in cash and gifts, each singer is sure to give the performance of their Life !

March 3rd PERFORMERS ARE AS FOLLOWS:

Robin Andre * Samantha Howard * Melissa Katz * Ben Poston * Ayana Bloom * Melodie Nicholson *

* Vince Robinson * OoneMic * Tenille Ja'Nae * ShowTyme
