About Me

Julie Titus

@naptimereflections

I am a married, stay-at-home mom of a toddler. The past few years have been filled with incredible challenges and life-changing events. As I stumble though them, I drift further and further away from basic self-care practices. I've grown mentally, physically, and emotionally malnourished. This not only harms me, but everyone in my home.

Naptime Reflections is my attempt to remedy this self-induced state of survival on auto-pilot. Despite the plethora of information on parenting and discipline, or the wealth of self-help programs at our fingertips, I have found very little quality support or information on practicing self-care in motherhood. My mission is to find and share the resources that do exist, put what I learn into practice, create solutions that work for me, and share my imperfections in the process.
