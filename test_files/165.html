5 Foodie Documentaries You Need To See

9th July 2015 by Cat 11 Comments

I can’t help but feel left out sometimes.

When I hear (or read) about all the ‘binge watching’ of riveting television series’ that everyone and their dog seems to be hooked on.

Ok, I just about caught the GoT bandwagon, but please don’t ask me who anyone is (bar the pretty one, the little one and my girl crush.)

Don’t get me wrong – I’m not trying to say I’m a tv-snob – far from it.

I just haven’t got the patience for it these days.

The one thing I do find myself being able to sit through? A good documentary.

Preferably on… FOOD.

1) Natural Resistance

At first I wasn’t sure where this was going – a film about wine? What could be dramatically wrong with the wine industry?

Ohhh… there’s quite a bit, I now know.

From the problems with DOC certification (hint: means nothing) to the impact that fungicides and chemicals have on the soil, my eyes were opened to the difficulties traditional vineyards face now.

I also loved the little clips peppered throughout – from animated shorts to Charlie Chaplin – sometimes seemed a bit random, but were a great compliment to the film.

Like wine and chocolate. A little random, but totally works.

I won’t lie – the opening 10 minutes are a bit gut-wrenching – and I may have had to look away for a good chunk of it.
