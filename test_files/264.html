Posted on September 12, 2012 by laurenta

Hi everyone!

Phew, we have made it through half of our first long week. I am already starting to see the signs of exhaustion… and am definitely feeling them too! I just wanted to write some updates to make sure that you understand some of the paperwork that has been coming home. Today, I sent home the permission slips for two of our upcoming field trips. One is next Friday, September 21 and we will be going with the rest of Summit House on a small hike/group building activity. The other is Monday, September 24 at Saint Michael’s College with Mrs. Sato’s 3rd graders. Please read both letters and sign the permission slips so that your child may participate in the trips. I also sent home the picture order packet for picture day.

Just a reminder, tomorrow is our LAST day to work on our Writer’s/Reader’s Notebooks, so please send in photos if you have not already. Also, I have not received all of the Edmodo consent forms yet. I need those as soon as possible! I will speak to the students who I am missing them from tomorrow and review the expectations. Please make sure that you discuss Edmodo at home. Tomorrow we will log onto our accounts and look at making an avatar photo! We will also start having our first discussion! On Friday, we will work on commenting on the blog and choosing our theme!

Thanks for all of your support in 3rd grade! As always, feel free to email me with any questions or concerns!

We made it!!

Posted on September 3, 2012 by laurenta
Reply

We made it through our first week of school! Wow, it was a busy one. On Wednesday, we had our very first day of Guidance, a new student assembly with Mrs. Benay and Ms. Millham, and played some “get to know you” games! On Thursday, we had our first day of P.E. and a bus tour to remind us of the bus expectations at Malletts Bay School. We also reviewed how to evacuate the bus safely. We started EveryDay Mathematics on Thursday as we learned about the difference between units and numbers. We also practiced sequencing numbers counting by 1’s and 10’s. On Friday, we started reading, Flat Stanley, for our Read Aloud book. We also did a math number hunt, searching for numbers all over our classroom! We had our first day of music with Ms. Clark. She was very impressed by our class! It was a great way to start our third grade year.

We are really excited about blogging in our classroom!! We just need to make sure that we have the purple form signed and returned to Miss Laurent. We also need the Permission and Release of Information form so that we can start seeing photos on our blog! Both of these were sent home again on Friday, so please check your child’s Friday folder to see what forms need to be completed. I hope you enjoyed your long weekend! Looking forward to another great week!
