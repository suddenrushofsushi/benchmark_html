  6. wildbill20056 Sabor Ambassador

    Basically it evolved to certain traditions, some of which might be hard to maintain this time:

    1a) The first to submit in the previous compilation chose the music for the next
    1b) This evolved to a voting/consultation thread where folks suggested tracks and we polled it.

    2) In the case of many participants, we split into two teams each with their own editor and their own track.
    3) A deadline was set for everyone to submit by. Late submissions at the discretion of the editors.
    4) Everyone submitted to their editor who then put it together.

    5) Generally everyone got their screen name on screen with them, optional location.

    We tried to keep edit/post production to a week. Hey presto.

    We just need the bodies
