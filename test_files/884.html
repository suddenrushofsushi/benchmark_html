Product Review: Dollar Shave Club

Posted on 24 Days Ago by freehudson

There are two types of men out there: the sleek, clean shaven and the lumbersexual. For those, like me, who prefer to be clean shaven or at least sans beard, we are always looking for the next big thing in razors and their accessories. I go through razors often because, at the first tug, I’m ready to throw it away for a new one. That shit gets expensive.

Insert Dollar Shave Club. I found these guys a few months back through Facebook and at first didn’t give them a second thought. Just another damn ad for me to scroll through to get to my next Buzzfeed quiz. I started seeing them a lot so one day I went ahead and gave them a click. I signed up, very quickly, and began my journey through their short and concise selection of tools.

So to start off, you select a blade from an assortment of three depending on the coarseness of your facial (or whatever) hair. Of course they range in price from $1-$9 monthly. Personally, their median option works best for me. It has four blades that seem to stay sharp longer than any other brand I’ve used. I shave about every other day and I am changing my blade every 5th or 6th shave. Whatever your selection, they send you a handle and 4 blades of your choice. The handle is reusable and you’ll get four fresh blades every month, or every other month because you have that option!

Then you go through their list of assorted accessories: shave butters, repair serums, aftershaves. Things of that nature. I swear by two of their products. The Repair Serum and the One Wipe Charlies are everything. The repair serum is this really rich formula that reduces any redness or irritation and literally smoothes my face out for several days. I feel like if i put that on in between shaves, I can skip a day or two within my regiment. Their One Wipe Charlies are minty life. I pretty much leave toilet paper out for guests and treat myself to them when I’m on my throne.

Pick what you want, insert credit card info, and you’re set. When I got my first package, I was enamored with it. It was such a simple, manly, easy going package that includes a monthly edition of The Bathroom Minutes; a foldable little read-a-long while you poop that includes fun facts, games, and other themed articles. I’m pretty sure that when I’d first signed up I intended to end my subscription after the first box, but here I am months later still using it. Like I’ve stated, their blades seem to last longer than most other brands I’ve used and being a black man, I’ve not had any issues with razor bumps or ingrown hairs. Results may vary of course.

I wish I had some cool ass offer to provide to you as an incentive to join Dollar Shave Club but they don’t sponsor me (yet!) so I don’t. If you need more inspiration, visit the site or click this video!
