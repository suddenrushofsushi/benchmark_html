My Blogging Beginnings

by Alison

 

I have a few goals for this blog. The first, most obvious reason, is to share decorating stories, tips, and ideas. Another big benefit that I am looking forward to is meeting other decorators/designers as well as people who are just passionate about interior design. (This is probably the most exciting part!) The functional reason behind it is to grow my interior decorating business.

I started realizing my interest in interior design when I started decorating my first apartment. At the time, my style sense was not fully honed, to say the least. But I loved making a composition and coming up with affordable ideas. I have always been artistic. I enjoyed dance and piano growing up, show choir and art in high school, and planned on majoring in graphic design in college. I love to draw portraits. Through a turn of events I changed my major to psychology, but found my creative outlet at home.

Since the birth of our third daughter, I did not return to my job and have had the opportunity to really engulf myself in the design world. Through pinterest and other avenues, I have been able to discover what my style is. It has been a fun journey so far! I have decided to move forward and make a career out of interior design by starting this blog and working on projects for friends. My goals are to have a design business, including an e-design option and to share a blog with you all to help inspire and connect. I hope you enjoy following along on this adventure!
