Marisol's Kinda-Sorta Fitness Regimen!




FITNESS - where do I start?! I get asked this question a lot! What is my fitness regimen?! To be honest, I really don't have one. I know, I know... WHAT?! No, I don't. :/






I have exercised since the day I started walking and according to my Dad - that was at 7 months old! Ha, ha... :) I come from a family where fitness is embedded into our brains from day one. My Dad is a Professional Boxing Trainer. To read more about my Dad - click HERE!






I don't consider myself having a regimen because it comes naturally. NO, I'm not bragging... I'm just saying that what I do daily is the norm for me. It's like waking up in the morning and brushing your teeth! Seriously... I'm sure playing sports in school & being in the military for a few years helped too!! :)






I love running, biking, hiking and other things like Boxing, Zumba, Yoga & Pilates. I love to do it all... I have a gym membership but I also enjoy doing these things outdoors. I'm a mom of 4 kids so running behind them is a workout in itself! Ha, ha... I'm all about taking the kids out on a daily basis to parks, museums and anything that will keep us moving. :-)






I could start keeping track of what I do for fitness on a daily basis but I'm sure it would be BORING as hell! :p All I can say is "Motivation, Determination & Dedication..."



"Be yourself; everyone else is already taken."
-Oscar Wilde


As far as food is concerned - I'm a Foodie! I do eat healthy but I don't keep track of calories and stuff like that. I believe that you can still enjoy the things you love as long as you don't overdo it and stay active. (There IS this one time I had an issue with weight... that's for a later post.) :p

HAIR... I'm a big fan of Wash & Go's! While working out during the week - I co-wash daily or every other day and do the LOC Method. On the weekend, I usually pamper my hair... Shampoo, Condition, Deep-Condition - the whole 9 yards!






Overall, I like to keep things simple but FUN!


 What are some of your favorite things to do for fitness?! 
Do you have a regimen?!


Until Next Time,
Marisol
