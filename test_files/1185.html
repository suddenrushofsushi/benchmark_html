High Waisted Lace Shorts



My weekend has been full of events and seeing friends! Last Thursday was my sister's birthday, so this weekend she had a party in her apartment to celebrate! It might have not been 80 degree weather, but I didn't let the weather get in my way of wearing my new Zara high waisted shorts! I could not wait any longer, I bought these shorts on St. Patrick's Day weekend and have been itching to wear them! They are superrrrr adorable, do you want to see? You're in luck! ;-)






Edible candy!!
Cheers! 



Blue on Blue? I was really happy with this outfit, it took me a few tries to finally find the perfect look with these shorts! This look is very classy and great to wear to a dinner party or even a night out! The white blazer definitely helps to make the outfit stand out. I also thought these shorts look best with a patterned or turquoise top. And lastly, these Zara pointy heels are just the perfect end to this look. Super sophisticated and chic. So happy I found these shoes on sale!
