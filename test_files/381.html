Babys Only
Organic ® Formula Reviews

Babys Only® formula is manufactured by Natures One®.

The company makes the following products:

  • Dairy Formula
  • Soy Formula
  • Lactose-Free Formula
  • DHA Fatty Acid
  • Probiotic

According to the Babys Only Organic ® website, the product is recommended for baby's 12-months or older. "For use with infants less than 12-months of age, please consult a health care provider (i.e. pediatrician, nutritionist, dietitian, homeopath, naturopath, etc)."
