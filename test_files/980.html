Every year, we gear up to attend one of our FAVORITE events of the year – Catersouce in Las Vegas! This conference, which focuses on innovations, excellence, and inspiration in the catering and events industry, is an invaluable experience for us! Not only do we get inspired by our peers, but we are honored to host demonstrations for our fellows and for the past 3 years we have awarded a scholarship for catering education to lucky attendees! A Bright & Bold Brunch Inspiration Shoot: THE FOOD!!!

By Angela | Culinary Crafts Events, Every Day Life at Culinary Crafts, Modern Wedding Style, Hot Tips for Cool Brides, Parties, Sharing the Love -Vendor and Client Relationships, Vendor Love!, Wedding Trends | No Comments

As promised, today I am sharing our food that Culinary Crafts created for this GORGEOUS brunch inspiration shoot! We really wanted to create something different (and delicious!) and decided that a small plates approach was the PERFECT fit! This is totally a big trend right now and we love how small plates allow you to serve your guest a variety of dishes, without them feeling overstuffed and unable to try everything! Plus, it just looks so PRETTY! Check it out!

Aren’t these menus and the tablescape awesome?! Thanks to the amazing styling by Attention 2 Detail Events, fabulous floral design by Flowers by Adrien, and the stunning paper suite by Pro Digital Photos! Not to mention the gorgeous photos by Kristina Curtis Photography!

Our very own Mary Crafts-Homer taking care of the final touches before the shoot begins!

Sigh. Now THIS is a breathtaking table!

We created three small plates and as well as two beverages. I mean, how excited would you be to get to be a guest at this table? I know I would be thrilled!

Our first plate was our small plate approach to a breakfast favorite: Steak N’ Eggs! This divine dish was a Cornbread Waffle, Locally Farmed Steak, Frizzled Leeks, drizzled with Chimichurri all topped with a Fried Quail Egg. Yum!

Next we have a simply CUTE pancake platter! This miniature Whole Wheat Nutmeg Pancake Stack was topped with a single blueberry and accompanied by a cube of Himalayan Pink Salted Butter and House-Made Cinnamon Cream Syrup and garnished with a Fresh Lime Wedge. Cute and delectable!

I bet these two couldn’t wait for the shoot to be over so they could dig in to the delish eats!

Lastly, we have a sweet end to this fabulous meal! Not only did we serve a sweet Raspberry Mocktail Shooter with Fresh Berries, but no meal is complete without dessert! These Mini Old-Fashioned Buttermilk Donuts with Milk & Pink Sugar Frosting were served with a cup of Amano Hot Chocolate, House Made Toasted Vanilla Marshmallow, and Garnished with Candied Orange Zest. So good!

We had such a great time planning for this inspiration shoot and couldn’t be more thrilled with how it all came together! We hope this inspires you to consider a small plates approach to your event – I guarantee it will please ANY guest! Happy planning everyone!

Check out more Salt Lake City, Park City, and Utah County catering at www.culinarycrafts.com!
