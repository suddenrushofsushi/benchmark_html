OOTD// Matching with my bedroom

A slightly different take on a classic outfit post...if you follow me on instagram (@beckieione) you may have seen last week that I've been admiring my bedroom at home and this has definitely been refreshed since I went home last Tuesday. It was only a flying visit but, I really do miss home comforts when I'm at uni sometimes and it was nice to have a break from everything...even though the trip was really to force myself not to get distracted and actually start my uni projects haha!
My bedroom situation is pretty up in the air as I currently live in 3 different places, so it's hard to get one of them exactly how I'd like it. Obviously, the one at uni I can't really do much with as we can't decorate and unfortunately, all 4 pieces of wood furniture in it are completely different colours, but my two at home I definitely try and make look nice. I suppose the advantage of having two rooms is that I've been able to make them both completely different, sort of how I dress really; one is monochrome, and the other is girly and pastely!
The one I'm in in today's post is the girly one and my outfit definitely matches it a little. This wasn't on purpose originally but it did inspire this post! The jumper is a new favourite from Missguided and I think it was actually on offer which is even better. I love the bright colour and I'm really into sleeveless jumpers, although, I suppose they do defeat the purpose.

Jumper- Missguided
Skirt- Motel Rocks
Necklace- Boots
Lip- MAC lip liner in 'Soar' and shade 4 from the Sleek Ballet lip palette


How cute is my colour scheme?! Excuse the big black wall hanging near my bed though, my room sort of becomes the storage facility of the house when I'm at uni haha!
