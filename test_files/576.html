Cant think of another Pun-kin for this one.

Pumpkins Biscuits

The term that i am hearing being thrown around lately is the word bagel. At the first experience hearing a classic frat star looking over at his not as impressed coffee date and proceeded to call her a bagel for her latte of choice (pumpkin spice if you were wondering). I could not believe my ears but had to continue listening to try and decode this strange compliment or insult he just told his date. In my head all I was thinking was he is most likely not going to get a second one. The eaves dropping did not help to uncover this mystery, so i did some digging on the internet. I resorted my detective instinct to the ever popular and most reliable source … urban dictionary. to my great surprise the dictionary of all this urban came up with nothing.

My next step was to ask a friend of mine who seems to always be in the know on what to know in the ever changing social lives of a college student. He helped me finally understand the meaning behind the term bagel being used around campus. And no its not just what you indulge in for breakfast with a little cream cheese spread it so happens to be a term guys came up with to stands for basic white girl. I understand cause basic white girl is just too long and needed to be shorten to use more often. ( to all my readers out there who don’t see my sense of humor close up I am completely kidding in my last sentence ). Boys will be boys.

A new kind of Hole-y Night.

It’s beginning to look a lot like Christmas…

Yes I know it is only November, but it is time to get into the Christmas spirit. I have put up all my christmas decorations and begun the non-stop concert of Christmas carols on replay.

Feeling guilty for the jump over Thanksgiving I decided to make some pumpkin donuts with a maple glaze to clear my hoildazzzee. I will have to say they were the easiest donuts i have made thus far on my holy food journey.

Also, I think I have finally nailed the perfect recipe for a good glaze. It hardened and did not seep into the donuts and make them soggy. (you can say i was a very happy baker today) These donuts were baked instead of fried and because of this they were made from a batter instead of dough. Baked donuts are more of the old fashioned style and cake like in nature and by far are my favorite.
