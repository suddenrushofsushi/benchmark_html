My fashion gurus! <3 Trinny & Susannah + Gok Wan <3

I love fashion since I was a little girl. I used to watch all the tv shows of Trinny & Susannah or Gok, and I still do. :-)

They are my biggest fashion inspiration. I love all these books!

I hate Victoria Beckham, she’s definietely not my fashion idol, but I just wanted to know what is she talking about in her book. That’s the only reason I bought it lol. She’s so nasty lol

My lovely Goku <3 He’s a genious, I support all his projects, he designed the new winter collection for Sainsbury’s..

So let’s start with Gok’s autobiography, really shocking. He had such a long way.. I was really impressed. He worked so hard for his succes! <3

‘The lost insignificant fat boy will never leave me. I am sure the bullies had no idea that when they poked, pinched and calledme names it would stay with me forever. After they had left me to cry, alone and unloved,they didn’t consider what damage they had done. But today I thank them. For without the jibes and hate I wouldn’t be the person I am today.’

‘When it comes to making women feel good about themselves, Gok Wan is a genious.’ Closer

Whether it’s creating fabulous new looks from the classics you already own, or investing in those simple style statements that never go out of fashion, Gok will help you to create a wardrobe that you can depend on to make you feel your beautiful best every day.

Style supremo and a woman’s best friend, Gok Wan knows how difficult it is to choose what to wear every day, let alone for special occasions. That’s why he’s written How to Dress and packed it with loads of ideas for great outfits for work, weekends, parties and holidays.

So girls, you’ll never have to worry about what you’re going to wear ever again! :-)

Trinny & Sussanah!

I used to love ‘what not to wear’! damn! :)

How to look terrific all the time, balance work, home, children and social life and still have time for yourself.

This book will give you total confidence to attend any event, work in any environment, go anywhere on holiday, and feel totally appropriately dressed, for you!

In this book Trinny & Susannah show how you can use clothes to look, act and feel different!

Which body shape are you?

Trinny & Sus have identified twelve definitive women’s body shapes to help women dress!

This book covers every aspect of presenting yourself in the best possible way, from skincare, hair and make-up to choosing the colours that suit you best and the accessories that complement not kill an outfit!

Now Victoria’s book

Victoria shares her secrets on dressing for special occasions, everyday wear, accessorizing, holiday tips and making the most of your wardrobe.
