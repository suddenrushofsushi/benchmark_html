Celebrating 10 Years of The Lightning Thief | #PercyPack

11

It was a different world when¬†I first read¬†The Lightning Thief¬†by Rick Riordan. I was working at a music store in the mini mall, right next to a Borders. DO YOU GUYS REMEMBER BORDERS? I was flush with minimum wage cash and buying books at Borders like they were going to run out (little did I know). In June 2009 just before I began my student teaching,¬†The Lightning Thief, first of the Percy Jackson and the Olympians¬†series caught my eye. I ended up marathoning it along with the newest Sarah Dessen, Lock And Key (and by newest I mean paperback because I was poor). I consumed this book on the sidelines while the current person in my life was coaching soccer. I remember Riordan’s book being the first middle grade since Harry Potter that I just really fell in love with. Later on, I would fondly listen to books in the spinoff series while driving around different schools in my county to teach students about healthy relationships. So, needless to say, Percy Jackson has played a huge role in my life.

I am BEYOND honored to be a part of the #PercyPack and to be able to give away this amazing box set of the Percy Jackson and the Olympians series to celebrate the 10th anniversary of¬†The Lightning Thief.

ABOUT THE SERIES

Percy Jackson is a good kid, but he can’t seem to focus on his schoolwork or control his temper. And lately, being away at boarding school is only getting worse–Percy could have sworn his pre-algebra teacher turned into a monster and tried to kill him. When Percy’s mom finds out, she knows it’s time that he knew the truth about where he came from, and that he go to the one place he’ll be safe. She sends Percy to Camp Half Blood, a summer camp for demigods (on Long Island), where he learns that the father he never knew is Poseidon, God of the Sea. Soon a mystery unfolds and together with his friends–one a satyr and the other the demigod daughter of Athena–Percy sets out on a quest across the United States to reach the gates of the Underworld (located in a recording studio in Hollywood) and prevent a catastrophic war between the gods.
