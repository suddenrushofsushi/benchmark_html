2015 Grammys Fashion – My Top 3

Unlike the snore-fest of fashion that the Golden Globes brought us this season, the 2015 Grammys brought some fun, glamorous looks that are actually some of my favorites in quite awhile! Here are my top three.
Rihanna KILLED with this sugary confection of a dress, and I would say that this is not just my favorite Grammys look, but maybe my favorite look ever. of all time. The first thing I thought when I saw it was Zsa Zsa Gabor – and I actually found this photo shoot that Zsa Zsa did in the 1960’s with a couple pink poodles. Spot on, right?


Jane Fonda’s outfit actually slipped under my radar at first – no one seemed to be talking about it. This emerald green and gold jumpsuit is playful and daring, while also being figure-flattering and fashion forward (take notes Madonna). To be in your late 70’s and out-dress most of the twenty-somethings in Hollywood gives her double the points.

Kim Kardashian’s glittering gold beaded robe is so amazing, I can’t even pretend that I’m not obsessed with it. It lends an Old Hollywood vibe that makes me think of something a 1930’s movie star would wear to a premier. The super-high slit and low neckline are perfectly paired with long, slouchy sleeves and pockets for a little mystique.
