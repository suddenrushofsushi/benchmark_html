Fashion inspiration:Week of Feb 1




This weeks fashion inspiration is Audrey Hepburn. She lead an important role in our fashion world. She basicaly invented the LBD [ Little Black Dress ] and made vintage in the 21 century the hottest accesory.

Date of Birth:4 May 1929, Ixelles, Belgium
Date of Death:20 January 1993, Tolochenaz, Switzerland
Mini Biography:Hepburn was a cosmopolitan from birth as her father was an English banker.
Trivia:Her famous "little black dress" from Breakfast at Tiffany's (1961), designed by Hubert de Givenchy...
Awards:Won Oscar. Another 23 wins & 24 nominations


Audrey Hepburn is this weeks fashion inspiration because of her truly chic fashion sense in Breakfast at Tiffanys. A couple of her famous pictures are feautred above. I truly sure that all would agree Audrey Hepburn is a role model for st woman/ teen in both sense of style, and in mind.

Books to indulge yourself in Fashion


Some books to further knowledge youself about fashion are the following;

"That extra half an inch" by Victoria Beckham

This book has many fashion/ style tips. It has ideas on where to buy differing parts of clothing and makeup tricks. This is the ultimate guide for a fashion lover/ a shopaholic like moi.

"Nine Heads"

I do not yet own this book, although i will soon. I ordered it from amazon for a low $60.00 [ previously around $110]. This is a book to buy if you want to follow a career in fashion design like i do. It clearly demonstrates how to sketch silouttes for your models and how to basically sketch a design. Very helpful.
