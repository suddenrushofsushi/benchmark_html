OTHER

  • There are flowers in my apartment, lots of halloween decorations, a concert on the books for the end of the month, letters out in the mail to say hi to friends, and Will and I have been talking halloween costumes.

Before I go, update on the salad I mentioned yesterday. It wasn’t on my “must make by the end of 2013” list, but it was a damn good one. Simple too! Say hello to my dinner for every night this week!

Frozen butternut squash sauteed in cinnamon and cayenne. Tossed on a butter lettuce + radicchio based salad with turkey, apples, walnuts, bleu cheese, and roasted carrots. I even made a dressing with maple syrup + dijon + evoo + apple cider vinagrette + s&p + tarragon. I made the dressing over the weekend in a mason jar so I’d have it for the other salads I’ll be eating as the week progresses.

Very autumnal and delicious.
