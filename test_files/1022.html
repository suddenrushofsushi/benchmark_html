Hot Mama Fashion Drama


I was recently sent this email from a very old friend. As usually I was thrilled to share some of my fashion advice. The email read as follows.


D-

Since you are now the style person.... I have a question.....


Is white OK to wear in March down in FL. I feel like it's not ok up
here...but I kind of feel like white is always ok down there. Like a
white denim pant or something.

I currently own nothing that fits me...it's all too big for a
change...so I have to do some serious shopping before I come down
there in March. I have a few top options that may work...but really
need bottoms. Just trying to build outfits and not just buy a million things that
don't work together!

I'm not a huge dress person, but I'm going to see what I can find. I
just don't have too much time to spend shopping these days with my
crazy girls. Also, you thinking maxi dresses will still be in this year? Or out?

Had a cute one I could bring the wedding weekend. I feel like Florida
just seems to have thier own set of rules, you know!?!?!?

Let me know what you think fashionista.

-A

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A,


Thank you for consulting me for a fashion update. I know you are a busy mama and while you may have fashion on the brain, it doesn't seem to fit into your schedule. Here are a few tips to help make your shopping experience a bit more enjoyable.

As much as I love white pants on a skinny gal, that look should be saved for summer. I think it could brand you a bit touristy if you wear a pair in March. May I suggest a skinny denim. Something stretchy that hits right at the ankle. This way, you can show off a cute pair of sandals or ballet flats. My favorite pair of skinny jeans right now is J-Brand. They are affordable and have an awesome fit. If you want something lighter, they have a ton of different denim shades. Save your white pants for June, July and August.

I completely understand your desire to want to mix and match your wardrobe. It's really the only way to shop these days! Since you have some tops already, here are some ideas to update your look. Key pieces for me right now are little shrunken blazers and billowy cardigans. Since you were blessed with height you should try something a bit longer. Like a men's wear blazer. I literally throw a blazer on over everything from cocktail dresses to a T-shirt and jeans. If you already have a blazer and you want to purchase something new, may I suggest a leather jacket. Something thin and super soft in black of course! Look for a leather jacket that is simple, so you can wear it for the next twenty years! http://www.4sisters1closet.com/ has a great leather jacket by Blank NYC.
Now, on to the dresses. I love maxi dresses! I think they are always in for summer and tropical places like South Florida. For a wedding weekend in May, I think that would be a great look for during the day as well as a rehearsal dinner. A strapless or halter maxi is a nice way to show off your tan too!

Living up north, as we both do, we tend to forget about color. So keep your options open and try to incorporate some color into your looks. Any more questions feel free to ask. I hope this was helpful.



XOXO
Dana
