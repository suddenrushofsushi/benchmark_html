WS : Thunderbird Resort – Rizal


My family escaped the hustle and bustle of city life for the weekend by going to Thunderbird Resort - Rizal.

Thunderbird Resort – Rizal, is twenty kilometers east of Metro Manila. Nestled right at the highland valley south of the Sierra Madre mountain range in the province of Rizal.It has 43 rooms that offers amenities for both corporate and social functions.

For Golf enthusiasts, you can enjoy a relaxing round of golf at Eastridge Golf Club. This is adjacent to the resort, which boats of a 72 par, 18 hole championship golf course.


Within the resort, is the world-class Fiesta Casino, open for leisure and gaming 24/7. You can find a great entertainment hub inside the Fiesta Casino, the Cabana Bar. Various talents perform great repertoire every night. This is also a place where you can watch the famous artists in the country.
