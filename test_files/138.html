To calculate Your Match, we analyze our secure database of 3.9 billion health care interactions:

  • We search among nearly all practicing doctors in America (that’s 893 thousand doctors!)
  • We factor in the things that matter to you, from the office location to the doctor’s gender.
  • We rank your matches based on the number of patients like you each doctor has treated. Our algorithms assess the similarity between you and other patients by condition, gender, and age.

Unlike many other health care websites, we don’t run pharmaceutical ads or promote “sponsored” doctors in featured slots. Amino’s results are data-driven and unbiased.
